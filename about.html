About Amboseli Lodge

For over 25 years, we've been the gateway to Africa's most iconic wilderness — where luxury and nature exist in perfect harmony.

A Legacy Born From the Wild

Founded in 1998 by conservationist David Mwangi, Amboseli Luxury Lodge began as a dream to share the magic of Kenya's wildlife while preserving its delicate ecosystem.

What started as a small camp with just 4 tents has grown into one of East Africa's most celebrated safari lodges, yet our commitment to intimate, personalized experiences remains unchanged.

Today, we welcome guests from around the world, each leaving with stories that transcend the ordinary — encounters with elephant herds, golden sunrises over Kilimanjaro, and the warm spirit of the Maasai people.

Our Values

Sustainability

We operate on 100% solar power, recycle all water, and contribute 5% of revenue to local wildlife conservation programs.

Community

95% of our staff are from local Maasai communities. We fund schools, healthcare, and sustainable enterprise programs.

Excellence

Every detail — from our organic farm-to-table cuisine to our hand-crafted furnishings — reflects our passion for extraordinary hospitality.
